The [PFAS Statewide Sampling Dataset](https://dataverse.harvard.edu/dataset.xhtml?persistentId=doi:10.7910/DVN/8LPLCF) is a compilation of sampling campaigns of CWS for per- and polyfluoroalkyl substances (PFAS) in drinking water. It currently includes approximately 98,000 samples from 28 states over the 2016-2024 period.
<br>
<br>
As permissible by privacy and data availability constraints, you can find replication datasets and codebooks for my work on the [Harvard Dataverse](https://dataverse.harvard.edu/). Replication code for my work is available on GitHub and linked in each associated paper.
